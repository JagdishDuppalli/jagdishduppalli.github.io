About Us

At Reyocto, we create IoT solutions for Aquaculture and Agriculture sectors. Our products / solutions bring technology and automation into everyday life of the farmer at an affordable price. All our devices are designed and built to work in demanding Aquaculture/Agriculture environments, enabling you to monitor, manage and control everything from anywhere at anytime, and assist in achieving higher yields, lesser costs and better quality of the produce.
